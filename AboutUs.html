About Us

Magandang araw! Welcome to Gunita — a home of traditional Filipino-inspired jewelry. Found by Zoey Alicante with her co-founder Elisha San Antonio; together they preserve the Filipino culture.

Our name, Gunita, means Remembrance. We chose this name to remind everyone of the importance of tradition. As Filipinos, we are blessed with a rich and meaningful heritage which is too precious to ever be forgotten.

That's why here in Gunita, we make sure that every piece is made with high-quality materials
to make sure it lasts long, just like the timeless memories of our tradition.
Not only that, our pieces are crafted with love, time, and effort,
making it not only a piece of jewelry but also the PERFECT gift for your loved ones!
